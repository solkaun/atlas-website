Who Is Atlas?

Atlas, a talented DJ based in Turkey, is known for his captivating performances and passion for hip-hop, R&B. Originally from Azerbaijan, Atlas's love for music has guided him on an extraordinary journey.

While pursuing his passion for music, Atlas remains deeply connected to his Azerbaijani roots. His background influences his creative approach, infusing his performances with a cultural flair that sets him apart. By blending elements from his heritage with the contemporary sounds of hip-hop, R&B, Atlas brings a unique and captivating energy to his sets.
